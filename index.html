Co-Typing

It is a collaboration platform for writing stories, articles, ... etc.

Click on the Rooms button to view all the avaialable rooms, then sign-in to a room using a unique username.

After that, you'll be able to collaborate with other users by typing in the same document.

Every user will be responsible to perform a specific task/tasks, for example; one of the users will be responsible to throw ideas, and the other user/users will be responsible for structuring the grammer.
